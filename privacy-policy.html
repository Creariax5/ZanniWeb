Introduction

Welcome to Zanni! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using Zanni, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We may collect information that you provide when you register and use Zanni:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Third-Party Login Information: If you choose to log in using Facebook or Google, we receive information from these platforms as permitted by your privacy settings on those platforms.
  • Contact Information: When you add friends to the app, you may provide their names, birthdays, and contact information.
  • Interests and Preferences: Information about your interests and preferences to enhance your app experience.

Automatic Information Collection

When you use our app, we may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Type of mobile device, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • App Usage: How you interact with our app, features you use, and time spent on the app.
  • Log Data: Error reports, app activity, and date/time stamps.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve Zanni's features and functionality
  • Create and manage your account
  • Process and fulfill your requests, such as sending birthday reminders
  • Communicate with you about new features, updates, and promotions
  • Customize content and offers based on your preferences
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities
  • Protect the rights and property of Zanni and others

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Your Consent: We may share information when you have given us permission to do so.
  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party vendors who provide services on our behalf, such as hosting, data analysis, and customer service.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • Business Transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Data Retention

We will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. When we no longer need your data, we will delete or anonymize it.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You may request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal information under certain circumstances. For more information on how to delete your data, please visit our Data Deletion page.
  • Objection: You may object to our processing of your personal information.
  • Restriction: You may request that we restrict the processing of your personal information.
  • Data Portability: You may request a copy of your personal information in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the security of your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
