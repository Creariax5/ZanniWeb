Data Deletion Request

At Zanni, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your data from our system, please follow the instructions below:

  1. 1 Send an email to download.zanni@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request"
  2. 2 Include your name and email address associated with your account
  3. 3 We will process your request within 14 days and confirm when completed

Once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. This process will remove all your personal information, including your profile, friends list, and any preferences you have set in the app.
